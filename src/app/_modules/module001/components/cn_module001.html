001 Content Projection with <ng-content>

HTML

Create Account

Login
Whats going on?
  • Module001Component calls <auth-form> twice
    • once with title Create Account
    • once with title Login
     
    
    <auth-form (submitted)="createUser($event)">
      <h3>Create Account<h3>
    </auth-form>

    <auth-form (submitted)="loginUser($event)">
      <h3>Login</h3>
    </auth-form>
  • In order to know where to place the projected content inside the form,
    the form needs a <ng-content></ng-content> placeholder. 
    
    <form (ngSubmit)="onSubmit(form.value)" #form="ngForm">
      <ng-content></ng-content>
      <label>Email address<input type="email" name="email" ngModel></label>

      <label>Password<input type="password" name="password" ngModel></label>

      <button type="submit">Submit</button>
    </form>
